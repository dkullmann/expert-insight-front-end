About us

  • Brandon Adams, Ph. D., CEO

    Brandon designed and taught three different undergraduate economics courses at Harvard before starting Expert Insight. He has written three books and served as primary research assistant for Michael Lewis on his book, The Big Short.

    Additionally, Brandon been on over 70 poker shows and is one of the biggest winners in live poker cash games held over last 6 years.

  • Jay Greenspan, President

    Jay has 13 years of experience in web product development and management. He developed web properties for Wired Digital, Apple Computers, Full Tilt Poker, and others. Jay is the author of threebooks and has produced and directed internationally broadcast television commercials.

  • BJim Patterson, VP & General Counsel

    Jim has over 35 years of business law and corporate experience. He hasheld positions as VP and General Counsel at Whiteco Industries, Heinhold Commodities, and he has served as managing partner in his own firm. He received his JD from Indiana University Law School, and has been a member of the Florida Bar since 1976.

  • David Kullman, CTO

    A veteran of high-tech start-ups, David has spent more than five years consulting with entrepreneurs and bringing their ideas to life. His wide skillset includes expertise in project management, software development, quality assurance, and support.

    Before joining Expert Insight, David ran professional services, IT and technical support at Fonality and was a project manager at No Limits Labs.
